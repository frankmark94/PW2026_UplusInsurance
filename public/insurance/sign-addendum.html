What you're signing

This addendum extends your U+ Insurance personal auto policy to cover driving activity on approved rideshare platforms (Uber, Lyft). Please review the key terms below before signing.

Period 1
App on, awaiting a match Your personal policy is extended to include liability and collision coverage while the rideshare app is on and you are waiting to be matched with a rider.
Period 2
Matched to a rider, en route to pickup Full personal-policy coverage, including liability, collision, comprehensive, and uninsured motorist, remains in effect from match confirmation through passenger pickup.
Period 3
Passenger in the vehicle Full personal-policy coverage applies for the duration of the trip. The rideshare platform’s commercial policy provides primary coverage; your U+ endorsement fills any gaps up to your personal-policy limits.
